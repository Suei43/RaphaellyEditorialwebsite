STATE OF EDUCATION AROUND THE GLOBE

UPDATED - AUGUST 25, 2022

Shift in the Quality of Education

Education is an age-old powerful tool. Its power has not diminished through the centuries. Indeed, it has assumed diverse dimensions. Research has also become the major means for the advancement of education across the world. As a tool for national and human development, education is considered a significant priority in all societies. The advent of technology, and indeed information communication technology with the development of apps, has improved the quality and volume of education significantly.
Education in modern times is beyond merely developing the human intellect. It is being designed systematically in advanced nations to equip learners with the skills to develop products, provide services and practically solve problems. Most nations attach philosophy, objectives and vision to education in line with the needs of the society. However, an enabling environment is created to help drive the ideals of education. Nations like Britain, the United States, Germany, France and Canada have made significant strides in the development of educational curriculum to reflect societyâ€™s needs and make individuals less dependent. Observations have shown the influx of Africans to these nations in search of quality education.
Movement of Africans, particularly Nigerians into universities in Britain, Canada and the United States is an indication that much still has to be done in the Nigerian educational system. The story is not different in some other less developing countries of the world. With the design and development of online education in advanced nations of the world, developing nations appear to be gradually embracing online education. This is made possible by the availability of the Internet facility.
