FAITH

SEEK GOD

UPDATED - DECEMBER 28, 2022

Man is a composition of spirit, soul and body. The reality is that the spirit of man is the life in man. Without the spirit, man is lifeless and out of existence. How did the spirit come? What is the source of man's spirit? The spirit of man depends on a higher spirit, and this is God because God is a spirit. Every human being has the innate desire to rely on a higher force or power in times of trouble. The entire universe is too well-structured for the rational man not to believe that a higher spiritual being is responsible for the geographical order of the whole universe.
Scientists, evolution theorists and free thinkers among others may choose not to believe in the creation thesis of the universe by God (the Supreme Being). It remains a rule that every human being must die at a particular time appointed, but God remains in existence for eternity. God's admonition is that man should seek him daily. Man is encouraged to have fellowship with God by the spirit.
The Bible admonishes man to seek God and consider him first in all things. God wants man to obey his commandments and follow him without rebellion.
“Who [God] will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.”
(1 Timothy 2:4)
Seeking God is making efforts to know his nature, ways and injunctions. God is good in all his relationship with those who care to relate with him. He expects man also to do good, pursue justice and righteousness. Are you willing to seek him today? The Bible is a recommended source. A commitment to seek God is the beginning of finding peace. If you seek God, you will find him, but you must seek him with your heart.
