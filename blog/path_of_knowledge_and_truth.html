PATH OF KNOWLEDGE AND TRUTH

LAST UPDATED - AUGUST 18, 2022

Knowledge of God

You will know the truth and it will set you free from lack of ignorance. Lack of knowledge is termed as ignorance, and ignorance is akin to bondage of a kind. Existence and purpose of man on earth are forms of knowledge that can be traced to the God, creator of the earth. The Bible is one great book that reveals knowledge about God. God expects people to find out about him. The Bible says that whoever seeks God will find him. Seeking God is therefore seeking knowledge. Whatever man finds out about God is knowledge of the reality of God. It has been found by theologians that whatever God says or tells man to do is the truth, and whatever he says about man is the truth. There is no lie or falsehood with him.
God himself represents knowledge and truth. But we are talking about finding out and understanding God’s commandments, laws, principles, statutes and injunctions, which if followed, will produce good life for those who choose to obey him. Coming to know God and his nature is a pleasant experience. God encourages people to come close to him. He expects them to seek him with all their hearts. However, people can only find God when they come to Jesus Christ, the Holy Son. Jesus Christ said,
“I am the Way, the Truth and the Life. No man can come unto the Father (God) except by me.”
(John 14:6)
Whatever Jesus Christ has said in the Bible is the truth about any situation and man. In the same vein, whatever God says is the truth. People may disagree about the content of the Bible or deny the existence of God, and give Jesus names or qualify him with all kinds of terms he does not deserve. Their non-acceptance of either Jesus or God does not change the truth neither does it nullify it. It does not render knowledge of God inconsequential. To seek knowledge of God is to seek wisdom, protection and prosperity. Anyone who embraces the knowledge of God and obeys his injunctions is counted as righteous, provided such an individual believes in the personality and lordship of Jesus Christ. This act of belief and acceptance is done in faith. There is a measure of faith in every living individual. Are you ready to exercise that faith in Jesus Christ, today? If you have already done so, then your faith can grow by studying the ways of Jesus in any version of the Bible, considered as the Book of Life. You are already on the path of knowledge and truth.
